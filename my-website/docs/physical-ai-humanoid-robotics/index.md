# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This textbook explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems, focusing specifically on humanoid robots that interact with the real world.

## Overview

This textbook is organized into 10 modules that progressively build your understanding of:
- The fundamentals of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics
- Mechanical design principles for humanoid systems
- Control systems and learning mechanisms
- Applications and deployment strategies

Whether you're a student, researcher, or practitioner in robotics, this textbook will provide you with both theoretical foundations and practical knowledge needed to understand and develop humanoid robotic systems.
